About us

OKTabletop is the next phase of OKDnD. OKDnD is a loose federation of Dungeons & Dragons groups up and down the Okanagan Valley that meet bi-monthly for discussion, help people get into games, volunteer at expos and cons, and run roleplaying events. Soon, we will be widening our remit to include many other types of tabletop games — including other roleplaying games, Warhammer, Netrunner, and boardgames.

Stay tuned for more details about OKTabletop as we develop.
